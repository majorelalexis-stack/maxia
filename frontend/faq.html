Getting Started

What is MAXIA?

MAXIA is a crypto trading platform with AI-powered tools. Swap tokens, buy crypto with card, use AI chat for price analysis, and access an AI services marketplace.

Do I need to sign up?

No signup needed to browse prices, charts, and the marketplace. You only need to connect a wallet (Phantom for Solana) when you want to trade.

Which wallets are supported?

Phantom (Solana) and MetaMask (EVM chains). Connect directly from the app.

Trading

How does the swap work?

We route swaps through Jupiter, the largest DEX aggregator on Solana. You get the best rates across all Solana liquidity pools. Transactions are signed in your wallet -- MAXIA never holds your funds.

What are the fees?

Volume-based commissions: Bronze (1.5% under $500), Gold (0.5% $500-5K), Whale (0.1% over $5K). No subscriptions. Your first swap is free (0% commission).

Can I buy crypto with a credit card?

Yes, via third-party providers (Guardarian, Transak, Moonpay). You complete KYC and payment on their site. Crypto is sent directly to your wallet. Visit /buy to get started.

AI & Marketplace

What AI services are available?

17 services including sentiment analysis, smart contract audit, code generation, wallet analysis, image generation, and DeFi yield scanning. Prices from $0.10 to $5.00 USDC. Browse all services on the Marketplace.

How does the escrow work?

When you buy an AI service, USDC is locked in a smart contract on Solana mainnet. The seller delivers the result, you confirm, and funds are released. If the seller doesn't deliver within 48 hours, you get an automatic refund.

Security

Is MAXIA safe?

We use on-chain escrow (verified smart contracts on Solana and Base mainnet), OFAC compliance screening, rate limiting, content safety checks, and SSL encryption. Your wallet keys never leave your browser.

Technical

Is there an API?

15 blockchains: Solana, Base, Ethereum, XRP, Polygon, Arbitrum, Avalanche, BNB, TON, SUI, TRON, NEAR, Aptos, SEI, Bitcoin.
